About Hrithik Roshan

Hrithik Roshan is an Indian actor known for his versatility, intense performances, and exceptional dance skills. He made his debut in *Kaho Naa... Pyaar Hai* (2000), instantly becoming a sensation. Over the years, he's delivered numerous blockbusters and critically acclaimed roles in films like *Jodhaa Akbar*, *Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara*, *Super 30*, and *War*. Beyond his cinematic talent, he is admired for his perseverance in overcoming personal challenges, including a stammer and a serious spinal condition.
